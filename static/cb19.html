Explanation

This is a database of timelines for Clan Battle 19 (JP wiki), augmented to allow you to (more easily?) select three non-conflicting teams. Each team is annotated with the boss, the expected damage, and a team ID that lets you know where you can find additional information.

  • CN: found in the Old CN Timeline Compilation spreadsheet posted to maincord
  • TheLab: found in the TheLab CB 19 Public spreadsheet posted to maincord
  • ... various videos from KR aggregated into a playlist by jwu89#5829
  • ... various videos from JP aggregated into the CB - JP comps spreadsheet shared by Xehanort#6829
