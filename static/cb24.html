Explanation

This is a database of timelines for Clan Battle 24 (JP wiki), augmented to allow you to (more easily?) select three non-conflicting teams. Each team is annotated with the boss, the expected damage, and a team ID that lets you know where you can find additional information.

  • CN: found in the Old CN Timeline Compilation spreadsheet posted to maincord
  • kawaiiclubmusic: found in the @kawaiiclubmusic CB24 global auto comps : global auto comps spreadsheet shared by kawaiiclubmusic#6076
  • ... various videos from JP and TH aggregated into the CB - JP comps spreadsheet shared by Xehanort#6829, with data entry assistance from Umbrum#6935
  • ... various videos from CN and JP aggregated into the CB - TLs spreadsheet shared by Xehanort#6829

There are also many teams that I didn't have time to do data entry for (and no one volunteered any spreadsheets related to them), so be sure to check these data sources as well if you're looking for teams.

  • various videos from JP/KR/TW in a bilibili playlist 1 and a bilibili playlist 2 shared on maincord by Jzzzzzz#3411
  • various videos from KR aggregated into a YouTube playlist by jwu89#5829

The Options tab allows you to enter in a set of URLs containing team data. Once those teams are loaded, you can select units you do not have and update various display options, as well as configure where the tool can check to find teams.

The column headers (boss, damage, team ID, timing) are all clickable, and clicking on the header will sort the table by that column. After finding a team you like, click the "choose" button next to a team to choose it, and the teams that conflict with it will disappear. If you decide the team removed too many good teams, click the "remove" button to unchoose it.
