Stamina Maximizer

Maximizing stamina is concerned with trying to get 336-458 stamina into your mailbox without wasting too many jewels. Usually this is achieved by sacrificing 210-250 stamina from natural regen, and it provides the following benefits over the simpler approach of only bringing 1438-1447 stamina into the event:

  • By maximizing for drop campaigns, you earn additional items from whatever the additional 458 stamina brings you.
  • By maximizing for story events, you earn an extra 2-3 memory pieces, saving 10-15 divine amulets.

Not everyone agrees that it's worthwhile to maximize stamina, with some instead preferring the simplier approach mentioned earlier. Both approaches are detailed in this tool.
